Hi. My name is Terry Lardner. I'm a self-taught Front-End Web Developer currently living in London, England.
I enjoy building websites and aim, as much as possible, to strike that fragile balance between building responsive, meaningful, usable websites and writing performance-aware, maintainable, readable code.
That is to say, I care as much about the design aspects of a site (stylistic consistency, typography, taking a hammer to that one pixel in particular, for example) as I do about the technical aspects of realizing it - efficient DOM traversal, minification, modularity, repitition, etc.

I also have my sights set on the full stack.
